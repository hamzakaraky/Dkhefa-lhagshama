Why 1 (Quiet Editorial) is recommended

The logo's hand-drawn restraint is exactly the editorial voice — generous whitespace, serif headlines, ember used sparingly as accent. Voice 2 reads more like an admin tool than a community front-door. Voice 3 is friendly but its rounded marketing-style CTA risks looking inconsistent next to the logo's classicism. You can still get dashboard density (voice 2) and warmth (voice 3) by *borrowing into* voice 1 for the admin and home pages respectively.
